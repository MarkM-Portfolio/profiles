Deleting people from your network

When people move or leave your organization, you can remove them from your network.

About this task

To delete a colleague from your network, perform the following steps:

Procedure

  1. Open the My Network page.
  2. Select the check box next to the person that you want to remove.
  3. Click Remove from My Network.
    Note: The person does not receive any notification to let them know that you have removed them from your network.
