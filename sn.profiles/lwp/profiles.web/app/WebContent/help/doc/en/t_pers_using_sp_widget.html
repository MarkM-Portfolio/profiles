Using the What Connects Us widget

Use the What Connects Us widget to find out how you are connected socially to other people in your organization. The widget displays when you are viewing other people's profiles.

Before you begin

You must be logged in to see content in the What Connects Us widget.

About this task

You can use the What Connects Us widget to view the social path that links you to a person when you are viewing that person's profile. The social path displays as a list of people who link you and the profile owner.

The What Connects Us widget provides information about how you are connected to the profile owner, and how the profile owner is connected to other people in the social path. This information can be useful if you are looking to expand your social network and want to find people who are connected to you in some way, or if you just want to get a clearer picture of the social structure in your organization.

Procedure

Use the What Connects Us widget in the following ways.

  • Select a person's profile picture to display a list of the ways in which that person is linked to you.
  • Expand the twisties to view more detailed information about how you are connected to the profile owner, or how the profile owner is connected to other people in the social path. When you want to find out more about the content that connects you, click the links provided to access more information.
  • When you want to find out more about a person in the social path, click the person's name under their profile photo to open their profile.
