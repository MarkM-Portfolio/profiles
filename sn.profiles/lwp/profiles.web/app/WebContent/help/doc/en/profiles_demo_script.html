This is the text for the Profiles demo

About this task

Use Profiles to search your organization for skills and expertise. Build up a social network of colleagues and track their latest posts.

This demo shows you how to:
  • Find people by name
  • Add a person to your network
  • Post a message to someone's board
  • Comment on a board message
  • Update your status message
  • Edit your profile

Procedure

  1. Find someone quickly by using the search control.
  2. Search results are returned with information such as location, job title, and phone number.
  3. Learn more about a person by hovering over their name and clicking to view their business card.
  4. The pop-up business card shows a photograph and links to other information about the person.
  5. Use the business card to contact the person using e-mail.
  6. Click More Actions to access additional options for contacting the person.
  7. Click the Profile link to display the person's full profile.
  8. The profile page displays information about a person, including job title, office location, and contact details.
  9. If a report-to-chain is available, use it to get a better sense of the structure of your organization.
  10. Click Invite to My Network to add the person to your network.
  11. You can also save the person's vCard to add them to your contacts.
  12. Communicate directly with the person by posting a message on their board.
  13. Enter your message and click Post Message.
  14. Scroll down to the Recent Posts area to view the recent updates the person has made in other features.
  15. You can access your own profile at any time by clicking My Profile.
  16. Take some time to check that your information is up-to-date and view the latest messages on your board.
  17. Click Add comment to respond to a message.
  18. Let people know what you're doing by updating your status message.
  19. Click What are you working on right now?, enter your message, and click Save.
  20. To update your profile details, click the Edit My Profile tab.
  21. Amend the field contents on the different tabs, and then click Update to save your changes.
  22. Now that you know the basics, log in and get connected!
  23. Thank you for watching this demo. For additional information, check the Profiles help page.
