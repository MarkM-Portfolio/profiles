Tagging other people

Use keywords to tag the profiles of people who have skills and expertise that interest you.

Before you begin

If your system administrator has enabled people tagging, you can tag yourself and others. You must be logged in to create or modify tags.

About this task

You can tag people using the Tags entry form on their profile page. Tags cannot be hidden or private. Users can check who has tagged them by clicking the number next to a specific tag to display a list of the people who added that tag to their profile.

To tag a person's profile, complete the following steps:

Procedure

  1. Open the profile page for the person that you want to tag.
  2. Type one or more tags in the Tags field in the left sidebar. Type-ahead predicts the tag that you're typing based on its similarity to existing tags.
  3. Click Add tags to this profile to add the tags to the person's profile.

What to do next

You can remove the tags that you added to another person's profile at any time by clicking Remove tag from this profile next to the tag that you want to remove. The profile owner can also remove any tags added to their profile by other people.
