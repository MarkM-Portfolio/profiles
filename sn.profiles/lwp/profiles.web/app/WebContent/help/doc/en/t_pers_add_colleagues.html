Adding people to your network

Use profiles to create a network of work colleagues and useful contacts.

About this task

You can invite people to join your network by sending them an invitation.
Note: You do not need to be in Profiles to add someone to your network. You can connect to people directly from the profile business card in any of the IBM® Lotus® Connections features. For example, if a person's profile is returned as part of search results, or if you are members of the same community as someone and you want to add them to your network, you can do so using the business card.

To add someone to your network, complete the following steps:

Procedure

  1. Open the profile page for the person that you want to add to your network.
  2. Click Invite to My Network. If you are not already logged in to the Profiles feature, you are directed to log in.
  3. Accept the default message or enter a message of your own inviting the person to join your network.
  4. Click Send invitation.

Results

The invitee receives an e-mail notification to let them know that you have invited them to join your network. If they accept your invitation, they are added to the Network area of your profile page and the Invite to My Network link no longer displays when you next view their profile page. There is no notification to let you know if the person ignores the invitation to join your network.
