What is the Board?

Every user has an area on their profile page known as the Board that displays the most recent updates for that user's network. Other users can post messages and comments to a person's board, making it easy for them to communicate directly with that person without leaving Profiles.

The Board displays the latest updates to the profile owner's status message and their most recent updates across all the IBM® Lotus® Connections features. This direct communication is useful when you want to keep current with a particular person in your network. You can go to people's profile pages and quickly scan their board to see a summary of their latest actions in Lotus Connections. The profile owner and other users can post comments in response to any update entries or messages.

Your administrator can choose whether to enable or disable the Board feature for users in your organization. By default, the Board is enabled. The administrator can also configure settings for the Board to control who can post to it. For example, the administrator might choose to limit board access so that only the people in a user's network can post comments to that user's board.

  • Posting messages to the Board
    You can communicate directly with the people in your network by posting messages or comments in the Board area of their profile.
  • Posting comments to the Board
    Connect with the people in your network by commenting on someone's status message or a message posted to the Board area of their profile.
  • Removing messages from the Board
    As profile owner, you can control what displays in the Board area of your profile by removing posts that you think are inappropriate.
