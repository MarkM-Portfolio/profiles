Accepting invitations

Accept invitations to join new networks and add connections to your personal network.

About this task

If your administrator has enabled e-mail notifications for the Profiles feature, when people add you to their network, they generate an e-mail message inviting you to join their network. The e-mail contains a link to the Invitations tab on the My Network page, where you can accept or decline the invitation.

If e-mail notifications have not been enabled for your deployment, you can access invitations directly from the Invitations tab on your My Network page.

To accept an invitation, do one of the following:

Procedure

  1. If e-mail notifications are enabled:
    1. Click the link provided in the e-mail invitation to open the Invitations tab on the My Network page. This page lists all pending invitations.
    2. Do one of the following:
      • To accept the invitation and join the inviter's network, click Accept.
      • To ignore the invitation and remove it from the Invitations tab, click Ignore.
        Note: When you ignore an invitation, the person who sent the invitation does not get any notification of your action.
  2. If e-mail notifications are not enabled:
    1. Click My Network.
    2. Click the Invitations tab to view all pending invitations.
    3. Do one of the following:
      • To accept the invitation and join the inviter's network, click Accept.
      • To ignore the invitation and remove it from the Invitations tab, click Ignore.
        Note: When you ignore an invitation, the person who sent the invitation does not get any notification of your action.
