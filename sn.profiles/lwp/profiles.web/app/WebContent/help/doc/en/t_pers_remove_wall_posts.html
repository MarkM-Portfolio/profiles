Removing messages from the Board

As profile owner, you can control what displays in the Board area of your profile by removing posts that you think are inappropriate.

Before you begin

You must be logged in to Profiles to remove content from the Board area of your profile or content that you have added to another user's profile.

About this task

Profile owners can delete any of the content added to the Board area of their profile page. People in your network or other users who have added comments or messages to your profile can only remove content that they have created themselves.

Procedure

To delete a message or comment from the Board:

Click Delete this entry in the top right corner of the message field.
