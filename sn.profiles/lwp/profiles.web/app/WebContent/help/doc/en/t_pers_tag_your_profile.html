Tagging your profile

Add tags to your profile to describe your skills, experience, and interests.

About this task

By assigning tags to your profile, you can let others in your organization know about your skills, job role, interests, and education. You must be logged in to add tags to your profile.

To add tags to your profile, complete the following steps:

Procedure

  1. Click the My Profile tab.
  2. Enter keywords that describe your job role, skills, and interests into the Tags field. Separate the tags using commas or spaces. Tags are automatically converted to lowercase.
  3. Click Add tags to this profile.

What to do next

You can remove tags from your profile at any time by clicking Remove tag from this profile next to the tag that you want to remove. This applies to tags that you have added yourself and tags that other people have added to your profile.
