Adding external links to your profile

Extend your profile by adding your favorite Web links to your profile page.

About this task

You can add your favorite social networking Web links to the My Links area of your profile. For example, you might want to include a link to your profile on the Facebook or LinkedIn Web sites so that you and your colleagues can easily access your social data from a single location.

To add a link to the My Links section of your profile, complete the following steps:

Procedure

  1. From your My Profile page, click Add Link in the My Links area.
  2. Enter a name for the link in the Name field. This text identifies the link in the My Links area.
  3. Enter the link URL in the Link field, and then click Save.
