Posting comments to the Board

Connect with the people in your network by commenting on someone's status message or a message posted to the Board area of their profile.

Before you begin

You must be logged in to Profiles to post a comment to your own profile or another person's profile.

About this task

The Board feature allows you to interact dynamically with the people in your network by posting messages to their profiles and commenting on the messages posted to your own profile. By responding to one another's messages, you can collaborate on events in your network and build up a conversation thread that is visible to others.

To add a comment to the Board area of a person's profile, complete the following steps:

Procedure

  1. Locate the message or comment to which you want to respond and do one of the following:
    • If you are responding to a message, click Add comment under the message.
    • If you are responding to a comment, click Write another comment under the comment.
  2. Enter your comment in the field provided, then click Post Comment.

Results

Your comment is displayed directly under the message on which you were commenting. The profile owner receives an e-mail to let them know that a new comment has been posted to their profile.
