Editing your profile

Edit your profile to add or update information about yourself, change your profile picture, or upload a pronunciation file.

About this task

An administrator initially creates your profile with information from the user directory for your organization. You can edit your profile as needed to add extra details and ensure that the information is kept up-to-date.
Note: The information in your profile comes from the organization's personnel directory and, because different directories store different information, the fields that are displayed in your profile might differ from the default fields. In addition, your administrator can automatically fill in a field to prevent you from having to fill it out. The administrator can also set a field to be read-only if it collects information that is not relevant to your organization.
To edit your profile, perform the following steps:

Procedure

  1. Click Edit My Profile.
  2. Depending on the type of changes you want to make, click one of the following tabs:
    Table 1. Edit profile tabs
    Tab Select this tab if you want to:
    Contact Information Edit your office location, contact details, job title, or choose a different time zone. Add or change your personal blog link.
    About Me Update the details of your work history and background information.
    Photo Update or remove your current profile picture.
    Pronunciation Update or remove an audio file of your name being spoken with the correct pronunciation.
  3. When you've made the necessary changes, click Update to apply them. If you amend the contents of a field and then navigate to another page, you are prompted to either save your changes or cancel.
  • Updating contact information
    Keep your contact information up-to-date to ensure that people can get in touch with you.
  • Changing background information
    Edit the About Me area of your profile to let people know about your skills and previous work experience.
  • Changing your profile image
    Upload an up-to-date photo for your profile from the Edit My Profile page.
  • Uploading an audio file
    Edit your profile to upload a recording of your name being pronounced correctly.
  • Adding external links to your profile
    Extend your profile by adding your favorite Web links to your profile page.
