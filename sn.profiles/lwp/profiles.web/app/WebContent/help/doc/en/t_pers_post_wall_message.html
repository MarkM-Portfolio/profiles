Posting messages to the Board

You can communicate directly with the people in your network by posting messages or comments in the Board area of their profile.

Before you begin

You must be logged in to Profiles to post a message to your own profile or someone else's profile.

About this task

All Profiles users have a Board area on their profile page that displays their most recent status messages and updates across the IBM® Lotus® Connections features. You can communicate with your colleagues by posting a message directly to their profile.

To post a message to a person's board, complete the following steps:

Procedure

  1. On the person's profile page, click inside the message field at the top of the Board area, where Write a message on this profile board is displayed.
  2. Enter your message.
  3. Click Post Message.

Results

Your message is displayed at the top of the Board area, where the profile owner and other users can respond to it. Profile owners receive an e-mail notification to let them know that a new message has been posted to their profile.
