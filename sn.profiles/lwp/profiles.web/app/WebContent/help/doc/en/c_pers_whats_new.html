What's new in Profiles?

Find out about the new and updated applications designed to make it easier for you to reach out to people and make yourself known.

The following applications are new or updated for Profiles in IBM® Lotus® Connections 3.
  • Updates to the navigation in Lotus Connections mean that you can navigate directly to any page in Profiles by clicking Profiles in the product navigation bar and selecting the page you want to go to.
  • The Profiles Home page has been replaced by the Directory page, which allows you to search your company's directory to find the people and expertise that you need. To access the latest status updates from the people in your network, go to the Home page and select Status Updates from the Updates tab.
  • Profiles now provides enhanced social analytic information to help you find out what links you to the other people in your organization and expand your social network. Use the Do You Know and What Connects Us widgets to help you decide if you want to connect with people and find out more about what you have in common with your colleagues.
  • The availability of the new My Network page means that your network contacts are now just a click away.
  • The design of the My Profile page has been enhanced. The twisties used in the previous release have been replaced by tabs, making it easier for you to navigate between the different areas of your profile.
  • You can edit your profile directly from your profile page by clicking Edit My Profile. The process for saving profile changes has also been improved.
